# Power Electronics Engineer
**Specializing in Converter Modeling, Design, and Advanced Control Systems**

Power Electronics Engineer with a focus on the modeling, design, optimization, and control of high-performance power conversion systems. Expertise includes **resonant converters**, **grid-forming inverters**, and **high-frequency isolated medium-voltage modular converters** for renewable energy (PV and wind) and electric drive applications.

---

## Technical Expertise

* **Control Theory & Strategy**
  * **Nonlinear Control:** Specialized in decentralized power sharing using nonlinear controllers for series-connected inverters.
  * **Grid-Forming Technologies:** Expertise in grid-forming inverter control and stability.
  * **Linear Control:** Robust linear control design for various power conversion topologies.
* **Power Hardware & Topologies**
  * **Isolated DC-DC:** Design and implementation of **Dual Active Bridge (DAB)** and **Resonant** topologies for power ratings from 100W to 10kW.
  * **DC-AC Inversion:** Development of 5kW 2-level DC-AC inverters, with LCL filter.
* **Simulation & Real-Time Validation**
  * **Real-Time Testing:** Expert-level use of **Typhoon HIL** and **PLECS** for Power-Hardware-in-the-Loop (PHIL) and Controller-Hardware-in-the-Loop (CHIL) testing.
  * **System Modeling:** Advanced simulation using **PLECS**, **Simulink**, and **SPICE** for transient and steady-state analysis.
* **Hardware Design & Embedded Systems**
  * **PCB Design:** Proficient in **Altium Designer** and **OrCAD**(limited) for multi-layer power electronics layouts.
  * **Firmware:** Embedded control implementation on **TI C2000** microcontrollers for high-frequency switching applications.
