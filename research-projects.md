* [cite_start]**Efficiency:** Achieved 96.5% efficiency at 250V DC[cite: 29].

## Grid-Forming Inverters & Fault Ride-Through
[cite_start]During my time at **IISc Bangalore**, I developed schemes to make micro-inverters resilient to dead-short faults[cite: 32, 35].
* [cite_start]**Setup:** 1 kVA back-to-back hardware setup[cite: 36].
* [cite_start]**Innovation:** Modified inner current-outer voltage architecture using impedance emulation[cite: 36].
